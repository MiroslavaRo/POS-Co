What is a black box cash register?

For catering establishments where 25,000 euros or more of the turnover consists of meals (excluding drinks and VAT), a black box cash register is mandatory.

A black box cash register is a registered cash register with a black box and ensures that all transactions are recorded immutably and in a verifiable manner.
